Risk Assessment Methodology

This methodology follows ISO 27005 and ISO 27001:2022 Clause 6.1.

1. Identify assets
2. Identify threats and vulnerabilities
3. Determine likelihood (1–5)
4. Determine impact (1–5)
5. Calculate risk score = likelihood × impact
6. Apply risk treatment:
   - Avoid
   - Reduce
   - Transfer
   - Accept
